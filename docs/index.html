App Features

Everything the
dunes demand

A complete operations platform built for quad bike operators and their riders — from first booking to final receipt.

⏱️

Live Countdown Timer

Real-time ring timer tracks each session. Auto-detects overtime and charges 100 KES/min — shown live the instant the session expires.

📱

M-Pesa Payments

Buy Goods to Till 6685024. Customer enters their confirmation code; the app records it and locks the session.

🛡️

Admin Dashboard

PIN-protected panel with real-time fleet overview, revenue summaries by day/week/month, quick-start booking, and full history search.

🏍️

Fleet Management

Track every quad's live status — available, rented, or in maintenance. Add, edit or remove quads in seconds.

🧾

PDF Receipts

Professional A5 receipts with full booking details. Print directly or send via WhatsApp — all from inside the app after each ride.

📅

Pre-Bookings

Schedule rides in advance with a date & time picker. Confirm, cancel, or convert pre-bookings to live sessions with one tap.

📝

Digital Safety Waiver

Nine-clause liability waiver with animated confirmation flow. Paperless, timestamped, and stored per booking.

🎟️

Promo Codes

Create percentage-off codes and toggle them live. Customers enter codes at checkout; discounts apply instantly to the total.

🔧

Maintenance Logs

Log service, fuel, repairs, and inspections per quad with cost tracking. Type-coded icons for at-a-glance status.
