App Features

Everything the
dunes demand

A complete field operations platform — from first booking to final receipt, built for harsh sun and sandy hands.

⏱️

Live Countdown Timer

Full-screen ring timer tracks every session. Auto-detects overtime and charges 100 KES/min the instant the session expires — live on screen.

📱

M-Pesa Payments

Buy Goods to Till 6685024. Customer enters their confirmation code; the app records it and locks the session.

🛡️

Admin Dashboard

PIN-protected panel with live fleet overview, revenue by day/week/month, quick-start booking, full history search, and analytics charts.

🏍️

Fleet Management

Track every quad live — available, rented, maintenance. Add, rename, remove quads. Swipe to set status in seconds.

🧾

PDF Receipts

Professional A5 receipts with full details, M-Pesa reference, and QR code. Print or WhatsApp in one tap after each ride.

📅

Pre-Bookings

Schedule rides in advance. Confirm, cancel, or convert to live sessions with one tap. Full pre-booking history included.

🎨

6 Colour Themes

Desert Gold, Ocean Breeze, Forest Night, Midnight Purple, Crimson Sunset, Slate Pro — with Dark / Light / System mode switching.

🎟️

Promo Codes

Create percentage-off codes and toggle them live. Discounts apply instantly with a strikethrough price at checkout.

🔧

Maintenance Logs

Service, fuel, repair, inspection records per quad — with cost tracking and type-coded icons for at-a-glance status.
